Services & Budget

The complete TMS service suite and a transparent, detailed view of our operational cost structure and projected income trajectory — presented with investor-grade clarity.

Mobility Services

Transport & Movement

Multi-modal transport options covering every journey type — from urban commutes to scenic exploration.

Flagship Service

Ride-Hailing

Our core transport service connects passengers with vetted, professional drivers for safe and reliable transportation across tourism destinations, city centres, and inter-suburb routes. Our ride-hailing service prioritizes verified operators, transparent pricing, and real-time tracking to ensure every passenger arrives safely.

Delivery & Mobility

Ride 4 U — Motorcycle Delivery

Ride 4 U is our fast-track motorcycle delivery and ride service designed for speed, agility, and affordability in urban environments. Utilizing motorcycle operators who know the city intimately, this service enables rapid parcel deliveries, food collections, and on-demand transportation in areas where conventional vehicles face congestion.

Last Mile

Cycle 4 U — Bicycle Operator Services

Hailing a Bicycle operator to render last mile services for you. Eco-friendly and nimble, our cycling operators navigate neighbourhoods, markets, and scenic routes that larger vehicles cannot reach — ideal for short-distance errands and sustainable urban exploration.

On-Foot Errands

Walk 4 U — On-Foot Errands

Hailing an on-foot operator to render last mile services for you. Our on-foot operators handle neighbourhood deliveries, personal errands, and community-based tasks — bringing hyper-local convenience directly to your door.

Professional Services

Concierge & Lifestyle

Beyond transportation, TMS connects travellers with a curated network of professional service providers for every lifestyle and business need.

Marketplace

Do it 4 U — Professional Services Marketplace

Do it 4 U is TMS's professional services marketplace that connects travellers and local residents with a broad range of vetted professionals. Services include concierge assistance, administrative support, personal shopping, event planning, translation, photography, legal consultations, healthcare navigation, and much more.

Platform Extension

Plug-in Services — Third-Party Integrations

Plug-in Services extend the TMS platform through strategic third-party integrations. Partner services include hotel and accommodation booking, restaurant reservations, event ticketing, airport transfers, car rental, travel insurance, and tourism experiences.

Safety & Finance

Security & Financial Empowerment

Safety and financial access are not optional add-ons — they are foundational to the TMS platform's value proposition for every user.

Safety Critical

Panic Button — Namola Integration

The TMS Panic Button is a one-touch emergency response feature powered by Namola — South Africa's leading emergency response platform. In any situation requiring immediate assistance, a user can activate the panic button within the TMS app and instantly dispatch their location and distress signal to emergency services.

Fintech

Payment Gateway — Vodapay & Nedbank

TMS integrates with Vodapay and Nedbank to provide a comprehensive, secure payment gateway that supports cashless transactions across all platform services. This integration enables users to pay for any TMS service using mobile money, bank transfers, cards, and digital wallets.

Revenue Channel

Digital Advertising

The TMS platform's engaged user base of active travellers and tourism operators presents a premium advertising environment for brands in the tourism, hospitality, and lifestyle sectors.
