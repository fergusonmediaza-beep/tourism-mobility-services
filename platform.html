Services & Budget

Mobility Services

Transport & Movement

Multi-modal transport options covering every journey type — from urban commutes to scenic exploration, from solo travel to group tours.

Flagship Service

Ride-Hailing

Our core transport service connects passengers with vetted, professional drivers for safe and reliable transportation across tourism destinations, city centres, and inter-suburb routes. Our ride-hailing service prioritizes verified operators, transparent pricing, and real-time tracking to ensure every passenger arrives safely. Whether you are a tourist exploring Cape Town or a business traveller navigating Johannesburg, TMS ride-hailing offers a superior experience.

Delivery & Mobility

Ride 4 U — Motorcycle Delivery

Ride 4 U is our fast-track motorcycle delivery and ride service designed for speed, agility, and affordability in urban environments. Utilizing motorcycle operators who know the city intimately, this service enables rapid parcel deliveries, food collections, and on-demand transportation in areas where conventional vehicles face congestion. Operators earn through a structured coupon-based incentive system that guarantees income regardless of trip volume, making Ride 4 U a key driver of operator financial inclusion.

Eco-Conscious

Cycle 4 U — Cycling Services

Cycle 4 U offers sustainable, eco-conscious cycling options for travellers who want to explore destinations in the most environmentally responsible way possible. From casual city rides to curated cycling routes through scenic tourism corridors, this service taps into the growing global demand for green travel. Cycle 4 U operators are trained to guide tourists, share local knowledge, and create memorable experiences. This service contributes directly to TMS's sustainability mandate and appeals to the rapidly growing eco-tourism segment.

Tourism Experience

Walk 4 U — Walking Tours

Walk 4 U transforms pedestrian movement into guided, curated tourism experiences. Local guides lead tourists through historical districts, cultural neighborhoods, markets, and scenic routes that showcase the authentic character of South African cities and towns. Each Walk 4 U experience is designed to create meaningful connections between visitors and local communities, support local economies, and generate authentic travel memories. Walk 4 U operators are verified, trained, and equipped with the TMS platform to manage bookings and payments seamlessly.

Professional Services

Concierge & Lifestyle

Beyond transportation, TMS connects travellers with a curated network of professional service providers for every lifestyle and business need.

Marketplace

Do it 4 U — Professional Services Marketplace

Do it 4 U is TMS's professional services marketplace that connects travellers and local residents with a broad range of vetted professionals. Services include concierge assistance, administrative support, personal shopping, event planning, translation, photography, legal consultations, healthcare navigation, and much more. For tourists navigating an unfamiliar country, Do it 4 U removes friction from every task. For business travellers, it provides the executive support infrastructure they need to operate effectively while away from their home base. All service providers are rigorously vetted, insured, and rated by users.

Platform Extension

Plug-in Services — Third-Party Integrations

Plug-in Services extend the TMS platform through strategic third-party integrations that enhance the traveller experience without requiring TMS to build every capability in-house. Partner services include hotel and accommodation booking, restaurant reservations, event ticketing, airport transfers, car rental, travel insurance, and tourism experiences. By operating as an aggregator for these services, TMS earns referral revenue while delivering unparalleled convenience to users. Plug-in partners benefit from exposure to the TMS user base and a streamlined integration process.

Safety & Finance

Security & Financial Empowerment

Safety and financial access are not optional add-ons — they are foundational to the TMS platform's value proposition for every user.

Safety Critical

Panic Button — Namola Integration

The TMS Panic Button is a one-touch emergency response feature powered by Namola — South Africa's leading emergency response platform. In any situation requiring immediate assistance, a user can activate the panic button within the TMS app and instantly dispatch their location and distress signal to emergency services. This feature provides critical security reassurance for international tourists, solo female travellers, and anyone navigating unfamiliar environments. The Namola integration includes GPS tracking, rapid emergency dispatch, and real-time incident monitoring. Safety costs are structured into the platform's operating model, ensuring this life-critical feature is always available without additional user cost.

Fintech

Payment Gateway — Vodapay & Nedbank

TMS integrates with Vodapay and Nedbank to provide a comprehensive, secure payment gateway that supports cashless transactions across all platform services. This integration enables users to pay for any TMS service using mobile money, bank transfers, cards, and digital wallets — eliminating the barriers of cash dependency that plague many tourism contexts. For operators, the payment gateway provides instant payment confirmation, digital invoicing, and revenue tracking. The Smart Jobs Coupon system layered onto this gateway creates an additional income mechanism — operators earn coupons that translate to cash or credit, providing income continuity even during slow booking periods.

Revenue Channel

Digital Advertising

The TMS platform's engaged user base of active travellers and tourism operators presents a premium advertising environment for brands in the tourism, hospitality, and lifestyle sectors. Our in-app advertising capabilities allow businesses to serve targeted, contextually relevant advertisements to users at the precise moment they are making travel and spending decisions. Tourism boards, hotels, restaurants, attractions, and travel brands can reach their ideal audience through banner placements, sponsored listings, push notifications, and integrated content partnerships — all managed through the TMS advertising console.
