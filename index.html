The Problem

A Fragmented and Inaccessible Tourism Landscape

Tourists and local travellers face a deeply fragmented mobility ecosystem with no unified solution. Multiple disconnected apps, inconsistent safety standards, and the exclusion of local operators create an environment of inconvenience, risk, and missed economic opportunity.

  • No single platform consolidating rides, deliveries, and services

  • Inadequate safety infrastructure for solo and international travellers

  • Local operators excluded from formal digital economy

  • High commission models exploiting independent service providers

  • Lack of financial inclusion tools for informal economy participants

The Solution

One Platform. Every Journey. Total Empowerment.

TMS is a mobile super app that brings together every mobility and lifestyle service a tourist or local traveller needs on one intelligent platform, with embedded safety and fintech capabilities.

  • Multi-modal transport: rides, cycles, on-foot errands, motorcycle delivery

  • Integrated panic button via Namola for real-time emergency response

  • Zero-commission B2B2C model empowering local operators

  • Smart digital coupons and payment gateway (Vodapay & Nedbank)

  • Professional services marketplace for concierge and lifestyle needs

App Features

Everything You Need. One Powerful App.

The TMS platform consolidates a full ecosystem of mobility, professional, and financial services into a single, intuitive interface for both travellers and operators.

Ride 4 U

On-demand motorcycle delivery and ride-hailing tailored for urban and tourism mobility. Fast, reliable, and affordably priced for both tourists and locals.

Cycle 4 U

Hailing a Bicycle operator to render last mile services for you. Eco-friendly and agile, perfect for short-distance urban journeys and neighbourhood errands.

Walk 4 U

Hailing an on-foot operator to render last mile services for you. On-foot errands, neighbourhood deliveries and personal assistance at your fingertips.

Do it 4 U

A professional services marketplace connecting travellers with vetted local professionals for concierge, admin, lifestyle, and business support needs.

Plug-in Services

Third-party service integrations extending the platform's capabilities — hotels, restaurants, experiences, and tourism partners seamlessly connected.

Panic Button (Namola)

One-touch emergency safety integration via Namola. Instant alert dispatch to emergency services ensuring traveller safety at all times, anywhere.

Payment Gateway

Integrated Vodapay and Nedbank payment solutions enabling cashless, secure transactions. Smart digital coupons create a new economic layer for operators and tourists.

Tourism Partnerships

Deep integrations with tourism boards, hospitality partners, and cultural experiences creating a rich ecosystem of curated tourism opportunities for every traveller.

Digital Advertising

In-app advertising and sponsored content opportunities allowing tourism businesses and brands to reach a targeted, engaged audience of active travellers.

The Reward

Sustainability, Growth & Financial Empowerment

TMS delivers measurable value across the tourism ecosystem — for travellers seeking convenience, for operators seeking income, and for the economy seeking sustainable growth.

Safety-First Platform

Emergency integration with Namola ensures every journey is covered by real-time emergency response capabilities.

Financial Inclusion

A zero-commission model and digital coupon economy opens formal income channels for previously excluded local operators.

Eco-Conscious Mobility

Cycling, walking, and shared transport options reduce the carbon footprint of tourism while creating memorable experiences.

Scalable Global Vision
